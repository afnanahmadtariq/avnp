# Product roadmap

Status: Planned  
Owner: Product  
Last reviewed: 2026-07-19

## Foundation

Initialize the pnpm/Turborepo workspace, application boundaries, shared contracts, pure domain rules, moving configuration, data model, provider ports, design system, documentation, and credential-free checks.

## Hackathon MVP

Close one moving-services loop: voice and document intake, user confirmation, three distinct counterparties, structured itemized quotes, one measurable negotiated improvement, and a ranked evidence-backed report. A deterministic simulated market protects the demo from provider or phone failures while live adapters use the same contracts.

## Demo-strengthening work

- Live negotiation timeline showing initial quote, leverage, new offer, and added terms.
- Savings dashboard showing money, time, and hidden fees.
- Golden-call fixtures and evaluation checks for fee extraction, disclosure, red flags, and truthful leverage.
- PDF report and shareable evidence summary.

## Product expansion

1. Auto repair, home contractors, and appliance repair configurations.
2. SMS/email follow-up and booking with the selected business.
3. Multilingual interviews and negotiations.
4. Negotiation history and reusable customer specifications.
5. Medical billing, freight, equipment rental, and B2B procurement configurations.
6. Proactive recurring-service monitoring and renegotiation with explicit customer controls.

Every expansion must preserve comparable scope, honest leverage, consent, evidence, and provider-independent contracts.
