# Demo script

Status: Planned  
Owner: Demo lead  
Last reviewed: 2026-07-19

Target duration: three to four minutes.

1. **Problem and promise - 20 seconds:** show the spread in moving quotes and explain that the user should not need to repeat the same job to eight businesses.
2. **Structured intake - 35 seconds:** conduct or replay the ElevenLabs interview, add a document, and show both inputs merged into one editable specification.
3. **Confirmation - 15 seconds:** confirm the version and point out that every call receives it verbatim.
4. **Discovery and calls - 35 seconds:** select three businesses or counter-agents with visibly different negotiation styles and launch parallel calls.
5. **Conversation quality - 45 seconds:** play short evidence segments showing interruption handling, honest AI disclosure, fee questions, and a structured ending.
6. **Negotiated movement - 35 seconds:** show the timeline from initial quote through truthful competing leverage to the improved price or term.
7. **Comparison - 30 seconds:** show itemized totals, hidden fees, confidence, business reputation, and the 30%-below-market warning.
8. **Recommendation - 25 seconds:** reveal the best-value choice, money and time saved, and linked transcript/recording evidence.
9. **Close - 10 seconds:** explain that moving is the first configuration and the engine can support other phone-priced markets.

## Demo resilience

- Keep deterministic counterparty agents and seeded results ready.
- Cache only consented demo recordings; never imply that canned audio is a live negotiation.
- Label live, replayed, simulated, and fixture-derived evidence accurately.
- Prepare a single-command reset that does not delete non-demo data.
- Keep screenshots or a short capture as venue-connectivity fallback, but prioritize the working connected flow.
