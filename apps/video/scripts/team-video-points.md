# Solo team video

Target: 25–35 seconds. Treat these as speaking cues, not a script you have to memorize.

## 1. Introduce yourself

**Cue:** Your name, that you are building Relay solo, and what you worked on.

**Example:** “Hi, I’m [name], the solo founder behind Relay. I designed and built the product end to end, from the customer experience to the calling and negotiation workflow.”

## 2. Explain the problem

**Cue:** Phone-based purchasing wastes time, repeats the same questions, and produces quotes that are difficult to compare.

**Example:** “I started Relay because getting service quotes over the phone is slow and repetitive, and hidden fees make the final options difficult to compare.”

## 3. Explain what Relay does

**Cue:** One confirmed brief, parallel provider calls, normalized fees, truthful negotiation, and a recommendation backed by evidence.

**Example:** “Relay takes one confirmed brief, calls several businesses with the same facts, compares every fee, and recommends the strongest verified offer.”

## 4. Close with what is next

**Cue:** Test real moving requests, expand provider coverage, and learn from early users.

**Example:** “Next, I’m testing Relay on real moving requests, expanding provider coverage, and improving it with feedback from early users.”

## Complete example take

“Hi, I’m [name], the solo founder behind Relay. I built it because getting comparable service quotes over the phone is slow, repetitive, and often unclear. Relay takes one confirmed brief, calls several businesses with the same facts, compares every fee, and recommends the strongest verified offer. Next, I’m testing it on real moving requests and learning from early users.”
